YouTube Comments Extractor

YouTube Comment & Sentiment Analyzer
Enter a YouTube video URL or search by title (e.g. "toxiix trailer"). The app finds the video, downloads comments, segments them into intervals, runs sentiment analysis, and displays actionable suggestions.
Tip: some videos may have fewer comments than 1000. The app extracts as many as available (aiming for 2000).
Examples of inputs:
• toxiix trailer (search query)
• https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ (full URL)
• dQw4w9WgXcQ (video ID)
