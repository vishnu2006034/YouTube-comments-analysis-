YouTube Comments Extractor

Extract comments (no YouTube Data API)
Paste a YouTube video URL. The app downloads comments using youtube-comment-downloader, removes duplicates + empty comments, saves them to a CSV file, and returns progress + final CSV download.
Tip: some videos may have fewer comments than 1000. The app extracts as many as available (aiming for 2000).
Examples:
• https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
• https://youtu.be/dQw4w9WgXcQ
• https://www.youtube.com/shorts/dQw4w9WgXcQ
